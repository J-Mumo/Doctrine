# THE COMPELLING CHRISTIAN LIFE (BECOMING A LIGHT TO THE NATIONS)
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ao60YKFnlZE

Isaiah 49:6 And he said, It is a light thing that thou shouldest be my servant to raise up the tribes of Jacob, and to restore the preserved of Israel: I will also give thee for a light to the Gentiles, that thou mayest be my salvation unto the end of the earth.

Our world will understand Jesus and the faith life largely through the quality of our faith life.
  Acts 4:13 Now when they saw the boldness of Peter and John, and perceived that they were unlearned and ignorant men, they marvelled; and they took knowledge of them, that they had been with Jesus.

  Luke 6:40 The disciple is not above his master: but every one that is perfect shall be as his master.

An imbalanced christian life, does not only limit and frustrate the believer (that does not capture wholeness and victory), but it creates a narrative that makes a generation fight God and fight godliness.

It will take beyond organizing crusades and evangelical campaigns to win today's world for Jesus.

## Pillars to a compelling christian life.
Compelling: Forceful, worthy of consideration, influencial

1. The strength of conviction. Your depth of persuation about God, the bible, the authenticity of christianity as a faith practice.

2. The power of character.
  Galatians 5:22-23 But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness, goodness, faith,
  Meekness, temperance: against such there is no law.

3. Resourcefulness and productivity.

4. Economic strength. Tha capacity to attract resources and use the resources to improve your life and advance God's kingdom.

5. Influence, gaining acceptance and attention from a generation. (Loyalty, accpetance, attention). The capacity to make men buy into your beliefs, values and ideologies without using force or cruelty.

6. Manifestation of the supernatural. Defying logic, defying science.
  Psalms 89:20-24 I have found David my servant; with my holy oil have I anointed him:
  With whom my hand shall be established: mine arm also shall strengthen him.
  The enemy shall not exact upon him; nor the son of wickedness afflict him.
  And I will beat down his foes before his face, and plague them that hate him.
  But my faithfulness and my mercy shall be with him: and in my name shall his horn be exalted.
