**Marvellous Light**

1 Peter 2:9 But ye are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, an holy nation, a peculiar people; 
	that ye should shew forth the praises of him who hath called you out of darkness into his marvellous light;
	
Light represents
1. Insight and illumanation into the truth of God's Word
	Ephesians 1:17-18 That the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of glory, may give unto you the spirit of 
		wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of him:
		The eyes of your understanding being enlightened; that ye may know what is the hope of his calling, 
		and what the riches of the glory of his inheritance in the saints,
		
2. Understanding: The ability to know how to apply knowledge in a way tht it profits you
	Acts 18:24-25-26 This man was instructed in the way of the Lord; and being fervent in the spirit, 
	he spake and taught diligently the things of the Lord, knowing only the baptism of John.
	And he began to speak boldly in the synagogue: whom when Aquila and Priscilla had heard, they took him unto them, 
	and expounded unto him the way of God more perfectly.
	
	Job 32:8 But there is a spirit in man: and the inspiration of the Almighty giveth them understanding.
	
	Having understanding is when it no longer becomes luck. When you can reproduce the results indefinately
	
3. Access to supernatural direction
	Psalm 119:105 Thy word is a lamp unto my feet, and a light unto my path.
	Psalm 43:3 O send out thy light and thy truth: let them lead me; let them bring me unto thy holy hill, 
	and to thy tabernacles.
	
	John 11:9-10  Jesus answered, Are there not twelve hours in the day? If any man walk in the day, he stumbleth not, 
	because he seeth the light of this world.
	But if a man walk in the night, he stumbleth, because there is no light in him.
	
4. Life
	John 1:4 In him was life; and the life was the light of men.
	John 10:10 ... I am come that they might have life, and that they might have it more abundantly.
	
	Romans 8:11 But if the Spirit of him that raised up Jesus from the dead dwell in you, he that raised up Christ 
	from the dead shall also quicken your mortal bodies by his Spirit that dwelleth in you.
	
	John 8:12 Then spake Jesus again unto them, saying, I am the light of the world: he that followeth me shall not walk 
	in darkness, but shall have the light of life.
	
5. Supernatural empowerement
	Luke 10:19 Behold, I give unto you power to tread on serpents and scorpions, and over all the power of the enemy: 
	and nothing shall by any means hurt you.
	
6. Season of showing forth, unveiling and exploits
	Isaiah 60:1 Arise, shine; for thy light is come, and the glory of the Lord is risen upon thee.
	Ephesians 5:13 But all things that are reproved are made manifest by the light: for whatsoever doth make manifest is light.
	
	
**Requirements**
1. Cultivate a passion and a thirst for knowledge
2. You must be teachable / meek
	James 1:21 Wherefore lay apart all filthiness and superfluity of naughtiness, and receive with meekness the 
	engrafted word, which is able to save your souls.
3. Determined to see the light of God manifest in every area of darkness in my life
	Engage the Word in every area of my life
4. Committed to the gathering of the saints like never before
	Psalms 73:17 Until I went into the sanctuary of God, then understood I their end
5. Committed to speaking the Word of God all through this year
	2 Corinthians 4:6 For God, WHO COMMANDED THE LIGHT TO SHINE out of darkness, hath shined in our hearts ...
