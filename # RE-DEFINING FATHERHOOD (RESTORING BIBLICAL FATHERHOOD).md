Fatherhood: It goes beyond being a male, married man, having biological children, family life.
            Only the male gender can evolve to be called father, but all leaders regardless gender can exhibit fatherhood qualities.
            The male gender is the scripturally authorized entity committed with the responsibility of fatherhood, but fatherhood as a principal goes beyond the male.
            Fatherhood is mostly seen in the family structure, but it is a principal that goes beyond the family institution.

Father: Hebrew expresion is Abba.
        Abba means source , foundation, sustainer.
        Greek expression is Pater.
        Pater means protector and nourisher.

Fatherhood is first and foremost a creation principle and a leadership quality that is seen in plants, animals and humans. It is about initiating processes, maintaining those processes and replicating / perpetuating those processes above one self.
The highest demonstration of leadership is fatherhood.

In the homefront, a father is a male who has accepted the role and the responsibility of starting a family, providing, protecting and building the children from that family until they attain maturity.

Fatherhood can be seen as one of the scriptural assignments of the man with respect to famly life, but it can aso be seen as a leadership principle that extends to all men.

When God wants to help you, he can pick you and plant you in a family.
  Psalm 68:6 God setteth the solitary in families: he bringeth out those which are bound with chains: but the rebellious dwell in a dry land.

## What qualifies a man to be called father with respect to family life
1. You must submit to the Father.
2. Marriage.
3. The previledge of bearing your biological or adapted children.
4. The willingness to be responsible in naturing and building those children.
5. The willingness to create a legacy beyond yourself.

## What is the assignment of a father
In summary, the assignment of a father is to provide leadership to his children and his household.
  Joshua 24:15 ...but as for me and my house, we will serve the Lord.

1. A foundation and a sustainer to your home.
  You are the one who provides the vision and pattern for running that family. Answer the question, where are we going?
  Proverbs 29:18 Where there is no vision, the people perish: but he that keepeth the law, happy is he.

  Every man should be able to articulate intelligently from scripture, a vision that drives your family.
  For example, "This is a famiy born by the word of God, with an assignment to raise a godly seed, that will become mighty, serving the purposes of God, demonstrating victory and walking in love."
  Vision creates the tasks that occupy people.

  Part of being a foundation and sustainer requires being a provider.
    1 Timothy 5:8 But if any provide not for his own, and specially for those of his own house, he hath denied the faith, and is worse than an infidel.

  Provider is not limited to food, the spiritual support, priesthood, is the first area of support, then the emotional support that is needed.
    Matthew 4:4 But he answered and said, It is written, Man shall not live by bread alone, but by every word that proceedeth out of the mouth of God.

2. A covering both spiritual and emotional
  John 17:12 While I was with them in the world, I kept them in thy name: those that thou gavest me I have kept, and none of them is lost, but the son of perdition; that the scripture might be fulfilled.

3. A mirror. The identity of the child should come from the revelation that the father brings.

4. All fathers should be disciplinarians. The formation of character and moral excellence in the children, in the organization, the definition of spiritual bounderies is the responsibility of the father.
  - Love people sincerely, with all your heart, but never become too connected to anybody, to the point that they outgrow your correction, it's bad leadership.
  - Never get too personal with people, that they feel in their hearts, that they are excempt from the rules that bring order. The day you cannot correct in righteousness, the day you cannot administer discipline, you have lost your fatherhood.
  Hebrews 12:6 For whom the Lord loveth he chasteneth, and scourgeth every son whom he receiveth.
  Proverbs 22:15 Foolishness is bound in the heart of a child; but the rod of correction shall drive it far from him.

5. Fathers are builders, mentors, coaches. 
  It is the responsible of fathers to teach children the laws of life, to teach them how to win and be victorous in life. Not just by watching your life, but by educating them.

6. A father is a bridge. The provide leverage, an advanctage. They give those under them access to connections, access to relationships, access to resources that they would not ordinarily have gotten to by themselves.

7. Every father must play the role of a prophet, speaking into the lives of the children and guiding them to find purpose.

Your fatherhood is not complete, until legacy 
  Proverbs 13:22 A good man leaveth an inheritance to his children's children: and the wealth of the sinner is laid up for the just.

### 5 things every father must transfer to his children, subordinates, everybody who looks up to you
1. Spiritual convictions. as touching prayer, your passion for God, consecration.
2. Your belief systems, your philosophies.
3. Your relationships, your leverage of connections.
4. Physical assets.
5. Transfer the blessing, the anointing that backed you.

Fatherhood is beyond a title, it is a call to respnsibility.
