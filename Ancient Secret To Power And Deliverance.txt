**Ancient Secret To Power And Deliverance**

Koinonia:- Communion - Coming together of two people
		 - Intimacy - A state of closeness that brings about oneness
		 - Partnership / Joint participation
		 
To enjoy intimacy with the Holy Spirit
- You must recognize and respect His ministry in your life

Components of true fellowship
1. Study of the Word
2. A life of praise and worship
3. Prayer
4. Corperate fellowship with the brethen

Rewards of true fellowship
1. Authentic spiritual power - Annointing for miracles, signs & wonders
	Acts 19:11-12 And God wrought special miracles by the hands of Paul:
	So that from his body were brought unto the sick handkerchiefs or aprons, and the diseases departed from them, and the evil spirits went out of them.
2. Multiplied grace:- Ease of operation
3. The release of your gifts/talents/abilities
