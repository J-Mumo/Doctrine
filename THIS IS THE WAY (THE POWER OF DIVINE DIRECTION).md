## Limitations of the human nature
Confusion is part of the limitations in all men.
  Proverbs 14:12 There is a way which seemeth right unto a man, but the end thereof are the ways of death.

Men are limited in knowledge.
  1 Corinthians 13:9 For we know in part, and we prophesy in part.

All men have the tendency for error in judgement and interpretation.
  1 Samuel 16:6 And it came to pass, when they were come, that he looked on Eliab, and said, Surely the Lord's anointed is before him.

The limitation of being an emotional being. Emotions and desires can be very misleading. Emotions can be hijacked by demons in pretencs of being the voice of God.

Limitation in sincerity. We often mistake sincerity for accuracy. Men can be sincerly wrong.

## Tragic results of following the wrong path in life.
1. The loss of time. Time is the unit of destiny. You always loose time when you follow the wrong directions.

2. You will be unable to access the provisions (supplies that follow vision) that are allocated for your vision.

3. Frustration and sadness.

4. All kinds of danger and ultimately death.

## Keys to accessing divine direction
God is ever willing to lead us, if we are willing to follow.
1. You must admit, that outside of God's help, you are limted.
  2 Corinthians 3:5 Not that we are sufficient of ourselves to think any thing as of ourselves; but our sufficiency is of God;

  Humility and meekness are principle requirements for divine direction.

2. You must invest in understanding the ways of God and the principles of the kingdom.
  Psalms 119:105 Thy word is a lamp unto my feet, and a light unto my path.

  Knowing how the kingdom operates can help you know when you deviated.

3. Pray passionately to be guided and directed by God. Pray concerning every step.
  1 Samuel 30:8 And David enquired at the Lord, saying, Shall I pursue after this troop? shall I overtake them? And he answered him, Pursue: for thou shalt surely overtake them, and without fail recover all.

  Psalms 37:5-6 Commit thy way unto the Lord; trust also in him; and he shall bring it to pass.
  And he shall bring forth thy righteousness as the light, and thy judgment as the noonday.

  John 16:13 Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide you into all truth: for he shall not speak of himself; but whatsoever he shall hear, that shall he speak: and he will shew you things to come.

4. Practice word based confession and quiet word-based meditation.
  Psalm 46:10 Be still, and know that I am God: ...

  Stillness in the presnce of God is power.
  John 8:6-9 This they said, tempting him, that they might have to accuse him. But Jesus stooped down, and with his finger wrote on the ground, as though he heard them not.
  So when they continued asking him, he lifted up himself, and said unto them, He that is without sin among you, let him first cast a stone at her.
  And again he stooped down, and wrote on the ground.
  And they which heard it, being convicted by their own conscience, went out one by one, beginning at the eldest, even unto the last: and Jesus was left alone, and the woman standing in the midst.

  Daniel 2:19 Then was the secret revealed unto Daniel in a night vision. Then Daniel blessed the God of heaven.

5. Outsource godly counsel from spiritual authorities and matured believers like fathers in the faith, mentors, authorities in the fields, men and women with proven results and experience
  Proverbs 11:14 Where no counsel is, the people fall: but in the multitude of counsellors there is safety.

  How do you know you need counse; when you pray and pray and pray, and it looks like the answer has not come.

6. Pay attention to dreams, visions and supernatural expereiences.
  Joel 2:28 And it shall come to pass afterward, that I will pour out my spirit upon all flesh; and your sons and your daughters shall prophesy, your old men shall dream dreams, your young men shall see visions:

  And he said, Draw not nigh hither: put off thy shoes from off thy feet, for the place whereon thou standest is holy ground.

  Hath in these last days spoken unto us by his Son, whom he hath appointed heir of all things, by whom also he made the worlds;

7. Outpouring of peace as a sign that God is with you.
  Psalms 85:8 I will hear what God the Lord will speak: for he will speak peace unto his people, and to his saints: but let them not turn again to folly.

  2 Thessalonians 3:16 Now the Lord of peace himself give you peace always by all means. The Lord be with you all.
