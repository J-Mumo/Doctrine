**Principles Of Faith**

1. Revelation: Brings you to a point of conviction/persuation. Knowing what it takes to make it work in your life
	- Study
	- Meditation - The process that makes a revelation become your own
	
2. Prayer: 
	- Brings strategy / instruction
	- Brings grace supply for obedience
	
Isaiah 51:2 Look unto Abraham your father, and unto Sarah that bare you: for I called him alone, and blessed him, and 
	increased him.
	
Genesis 12:1-2 Now the Lord had said unto Abram, Get thee out of thy country, and from thy kindred, and from thy father's 
	house, unto a land that I will shew thee:
	And I will make of thee a great nation, and I will bless thee, and make thy name great; and thou shalt be a blessing:
	
Faith is the action you take based on your conviction of the Word and inline with the instructions required by God.
